Our Story

Where craft
meets flavour

At The Fillet, we believe that a great meal is more than sustenance — it is ceremony. Located on Outland Road, Zimbabwe, we bring the timeless art of premium steakhouse dining to life in every cut, every flame, every plate.

Our kitchen sources only the finest local and regional produce, working with trusted suppliers to deliver cuts of exceptional quality.

Whether you arrive for breakfast, lunch, or a memorable dinner, we are open to welcome you every single day, from 7 in the morning until 9 in the evening.
